Buy/Sell Gold

GOLD crypto asset is a token from the Mint blockchain. GOLD token price is always tied to the price of 1 troy ounce of 24K gold on the London Metal Exchange (LME). You can mine GOLD if you have MNT(P) tokens.

If you want to make operations with GOLD, you will need to approve your address in Mint blockchain by completing KYC flow, because we are selling GOLD only to authorized users.

You can swap your GOLD token to the physical gold in the Custody Bot or by ordering delivery.
